I. Abstract

Web Gardening is a web application that serves as a digital assistance in the growth of spring flowers. It offers users a variety of resources and knowledge to help them properly care for plants, as well as an interactive experience to help them develop their gardening skills.

The application allows users to manage their own crops as well as purchase flowers. The program will receive data of interest from a range of sensors, such as soil moisture, ambient temperature, and a stream of photos of the monitored plants' progress. The program should recommend irrigation activities, temperature and/or brightness adjustments, and harvesting based on the sensor values and the growth period of the farmed plant.

Authors: Roman Paula, Donea Emilia and Vartolaș Timeea

II. Introduction

Web Gardening is a project that focuses on producing a web application that provides users with suggestions and tools for caring for their gardens and spring plants while also allowing them to explore and discover a wide variety of flowers that they can purchase.

Authors: Roman Paula, Donea Emilia and Vartolaș Timeea

2.1 The application's intended customers

Web Gardening is a web program that provides suggestions and resources to gardeners and plant enthusiasts for caring for their spring flowers. Our app's target users are gardeners and plant lovers, as well as customers looking to expand their plant collection with new and beautiful blooms.

For gadeners, the app provides enhanced tools for monitoring plant growth and identifying potential problems . Gardeners can create their own crops and manage them online using the application.

Our website includes a number of features and tools for plant and flower enthusiasts, including those wishing to purchase plants or flowers. Buyers can register to purchase a plant through the app, and they will be notified through email when the flower blooms. They can also add things to their basket, post reviews, and receive plant care advice and ideas.

Authors: Roman Paula, Donea Emilia and Vartolaș Timeea

2.2 Purpose

The app purpose is to make gardening more accessible and enjoyable for a wider range of people, and to provide a platform for gardening enthusiasts to connect and share their knowledge and experiences.

Authors: Roman Paula, Donea Emilia and Vartolaș Timeea

2.3 Vision

This app's vision is to foster an active network of gardening enthusiasts who can connect, share tips and ideas, and learn from one another. By providing a user-friendly design and a wide range of resources, such as plant care manuals,  and a marketplace for buying and selling gardening materials, the app intends to make gardening more accessible and approachable for everyone, regardless of their level of skill.

Authors: Roman Paula, Donea Emilia and Vartolaș Timeea

III. Structure

3.1 Application theme (colors)

Green and yellow are often associated with nature and plants, which is relevant to the theme of our application focused on gardening and flowers. Additionally, green is often used to represent growth, freshness, and vitality, while yellow is associated with happiness, optimism, and warmth. By combining these colors, we aimed to create a visually appealing and inviting design that reflects the essence of our application.

Authors: Roman Paula, Donea Emilia and Vartolaș Timeea

3.2 Title

The term "Plant Palooza" was chosen to connote a lively and exciting atmosphere surrounding the world of gardening, particularly the growing of spring flowers. "Palooza" is an American slang phrase for a large event that is full of energy and fun, and is frequently linked with music and entertainment. As a result, "Plant Palooza" connotes a flower and gardening party where the community can come to learn, share knowledge, and enjoy the experience of growing plants and flowers.

Authors: Roman Paula, Donea Emilia and Vartolaș Timeea

3.3 Logo

The app's logo is a sign of gardening and plant growth, implying to users that the app would be useful in their task of cultivating and caring for plants. The wheelbarrow image also emphasizes the time and effort required for plant care and gardening. The app's tagline, "your one stop shop for all things floral," implies that it is a one-stop shop for all your plant and flower needs, allowing users to find and purchase anything they need to improve their gardens and satisfy their passion for flowers.

Authors: Roman Paula

3.8 Login and Register

The login page is thoughtfully designed to attract users to create an account on our application. The background video depicts a beautiful greenhouse where gardeners grow plants and flowers in a peaceful and tranquil environment. The soothing sound of raindrops could evoke the feeling of a light drizzle or sprinkler spray, adding to the overall calming effect. A jar of seeds is also visible, hinting at the potential for growth and development. 3.10 Cart

Currently, the shopping cart feature is designed to be simple and user-friendly, with a neat menu that opens up when the cursor is moved over it. The menu displays the products that have been added to the cart. However, to further enhance the user's experience, it could be beneficial to create a dedicated Shopping Cart page. This page would provide additional details such as the quantity of products in the cart, along with a more detailed overview of each product.

Authors: Roman Paula

3.11 Notification

As previously mentioned with the shopping cart, the notifications menu also features a clean and user-friendly interface that pops up when the user hovers over the notifications button. However, to further enhance user experience and efficiency, we could consider creating a dedicated notification page where users can easily view and manage promotions and messages they have received.

Authors: Roman Paula

IV.The users and their roles in the application

4.1 User

A user/buyer can use the application in several ways, such as browsing the available products in the shop, adding products to the cart, checking out, and leaving reviews.

The user can create an account and save their preferences, purchase history, and personal information. Furthermore, the user can access the notification system that displays any new promotions or messages from the application. The help center is also available, where the user can find answers to frequently asked questions. In summary, the user can use the application to browse, purchase, and review products, access plant care information, connect with other gardeners, manage their account, and receive notifications and support.

Authors: Roman Paula, Donea Emilia and Vartolaș Timeea

4.2 Gardener

In Plant Palooza, gardeners have the ability to access and monitor their crops at any time of the day, checking for brightness, heat, and humidity levels. Additionally, they can add new crops to their inventory and post flowers for purchase. The application also provides valuable advice for crop care, such as irrigation, soil type, and water management.

Authors: Roman Paula, Donea Emilia and Vartolaș Timeea

4.3 Administrator - Bonus

An administrator in the Plant Palooza application has access to various features that allow them to manage the platform efficiently. They can add or remove products, manage user accounts, and monitor transactions.

Authors: Roman Paula, Donea Emilia and Vartolaș Timeea
