FREQUENT QUESTIONS:

How do I track my flower delivery?
To track your flower delivery, log in to your account, go to the "My Orders" section, select the relevant order, and click on the option to track the order.
What if my flowers arrive damaged?
If the flowers arrive dameged, you can return them and the money will be returned to you in a maximum of 7 days.
What if I need to cancel or change my order?
If the order has not yet been sent to the courier (you can see that in my order status), then you can cancel or change the order. But if it has already been sent to the courier, after it reaches you, you can return it.
How can I contact customer support if I have a question or concern about my order?
You can call at the following phone numbers: 0768586645 or 0333954793
How do I know if my order was successfully placed?
You will receive a confirmation email once your order has been successfully placed.
